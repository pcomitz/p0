Tony Rice

Originally the guitar player for JD Crowe and the New South . Tony Rice grew into the standard bearer for flatpicking guitar. Tony Rice plays Clarence White's Martin D-28, serial number 58957. The header for all pages on this site is that Martin D-28 guitar made by CF Martin and Company in 1925.

Enjoyed a succesful solo career for many years. Established a wide base of devoted fans and listeners. Tony Rice is the standard by which all modern flatpickers are judgded. Tony Rice pushed the limits of the flatpicking guitar style by recording and performing jazz classics such as My Favorite Things bu John Coltrane and Four on Six by Wes Mongomerty.
